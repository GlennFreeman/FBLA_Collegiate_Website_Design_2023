Taco Tuesday

Taco Tuesday

4.4.23 | 11:00AM - 3:00PM

A food truck event that celebrates everyone's favorite day of the week with a delicious Mexican twist. This event is all about bringing the best tacos in town to one location, so you can indulge in a variety of flavors and styles. From traditional beef and chicken tacos to vegan and vegetarian options, there's something for everyone. Enjoy your tacos with fresh salsa, guacamole, and a variety of toppings, and wash them down with a cold margarita or horchata. So come join us on Tuesday for a fun and tasty evening that will satisfy your craving for Mexican food!

About Us

Howdy! I am Marco and I'm the owner of Papa's Pizza, a mobile food truck that serves up the most delicious and authentic pizza around. At Papa's Pizza, we are passionate about using only the finest ingredients in our pizzas. We source the freshest produce and the highest quality meats to ensure that every bite is bursting with flavor. Our dough is made fresh daily, and we only use premium, whole milk mozzarella cheese for our toppings.

So if you're looking for a pizza that's made with love and care, come visit us at Papa's Pizza. We promise that once you try our pizza, you won't want to go anywhere else.

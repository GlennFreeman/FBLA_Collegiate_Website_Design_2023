About Us

Hi there, my name is Jin and I am the owner of Seoul On Wheels, a mobile food truck that specializes in authentic Korean cuisine. At Seoul On Wheels, we are passionate about sharing the rich flavors and traditions of Korean food with our customers. We use only the freshest ingredients and authentic recipes to create dishes that are bursting with flavor.

At Seoul On Wheels, we take pride in providing a welcoming and friendly environment for our customers. We believe that food is a universal language that brings people together, and we love to share our passion for Korean cuisine with our customers.

So whether you're a fan of Korean food or just looking to try something new, stop by Seoul On Wheels and experience the delicious flavors of Korea. We can't wait to share our love of Korean cuisine with you!

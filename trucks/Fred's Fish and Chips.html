About Us

Hello! My name is Fred, and I'm the proud owner of Fred's Fish and Chips, a mobile food truck that's all about serving up the best fish and chips around.

At Fred's Fish and Chips, we believe that good food starts with good ingredients. That's why we source only the freshest seafood and potatoes for our dishes. Our fish is delivered fresh daily, and we only use the highest quality potatoes for our fries.

We take pride in our cooking process as well. Our fish is hand-battered and fried to perfection, and our fries are cut and cooked on-site for maximum freshness. We believe that this attention to detail is what sets us apart and keeps our customers coming back for more.

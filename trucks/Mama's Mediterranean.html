About Us

My name is Maria, and I'm the owner of Mama's Mediterranean, a mobile food truck that's all about serving up the most delicious Mediterranean cuisine around.

At Mama's Mediterranean, we believe that good food starts with fresh and authentic ingredients. That's why we source only the finest spices and ingredients from local markets and suppliers. We're committed to using fresh produce and meats in our dishes, and we never use any artificial preservatives or additives.

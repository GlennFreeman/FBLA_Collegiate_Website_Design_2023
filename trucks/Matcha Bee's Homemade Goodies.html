About Us

Hi there, my name is Bee and I am the owner of Matcha Bee's Homemade Goodies, a mobile bakery food truck that specializes in homemade baked goods with a unique twist. At Matcha Bee's, we believe in using only the finest and freshest ingredients in all of our baked goods. We use locally sourced ingredients whenever possible and we never use any preservatives or artificial flavorings.

At Matcha Bee's, we take pride in creating a warm and welcoming environment for our customers. We believe that baking is an art form and we love to share our passion for baking with our customers. Our goal is to provide delicious baked goods that are not only visually appealing but taste amazing too.

So whether you're in the mood for something sweet or savory, stop by Matcha Bee's Homemade Goodies and try our delicious baked goods. We promise you won't be disappointed!

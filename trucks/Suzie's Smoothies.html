About Us

My name is Suzie, and I'm the owner of Suzie's Smoothies, a mobile smoothie truck that's all about serving up delicious and healthy drinks on-the-go.

I started Suzie's Smoothies because I wanted to share my passion for healthy living with others. I've always loved smoothies and the way they make me feel, so I decided to create a mobile smoothie truck that would allow me to share this passion with others.

At Suzie's Smoothies, we're committed to using only the freshest and healthiest ingredients. We source our fruits and vegetables from local farms, and we never add any artificial flavors or preservatives to our drinks. All of our smoothies are made-to-order, so you can be sure that you're getting the freshest and healthiest drink possible.

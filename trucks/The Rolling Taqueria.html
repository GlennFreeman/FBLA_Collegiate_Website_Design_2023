About Us

When I first started The Rolling Taqueria, I had no idea how much it would grow and become such a beloved staple in the community. I had always been passionate about Mexican food, and I knew that I wanted to bring authentic and delicious tacos to people on-the-go.

Starting out was tough. I had to work long hours, sometimes waking up before the sun and staying up late into the night, just to make sure that everything was prepared fresh and ready for the day. But seeing the smiles on my customers' faces made it all worth it.

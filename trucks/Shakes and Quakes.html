About Us

Hello there, my name is Alex and I am the owner of Shakes and Quakes, a mobile food truck that specializes in classic American diner fare and milkshakes. At Shakes and Quakes, we pride ourselves on offering delicious comfort food that is sure to satisfy your cravings.

At Shakes and Quakes, we believe that good food brings people together, and we strive to create a welcoming and friendly atmosphere for our customers. We take pride in serving up delicious food that is made with care and attention to detail.

So whether you're in the mood for a classic cheeseburger and fries or want to indulge in a delicious milkshake, stop by Shakes and Quakes and experience the taste of classic American diner fare. We promise you won't be disappointed!

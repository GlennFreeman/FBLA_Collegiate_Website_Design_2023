About Us

Hello there, my name is Sophie and I am the owner of The Crepe Cart, a mobile food truck that specializes in making delicious crepes. At The Crepe Cart, we believe that crepes are a versatile and delicious meal that can be enjoyed any time of day.

At The Crepe Cart, we take pride in providing a warm and friendly environment for our customers. We believe that good food should be enjoyed with good company, and we love to see the joy on our customers' faces when they bite into one of our delicious crepes.

So whether you're in the mood for a sweet or savory crepe, stop by The Crepe Cart and indulge in a delicious meal. We promise you won't be disappointed!

About Us

Hello there, my name is Mark and I am the owner of The Grilled Cheese Co., a mobile food truck that specializes in making delicious grilled cheese sandwiches. At The Grilled Cheese Co., we believe that a classic grilled cheese sandwich can be elevated to a whole new level with high-quality ingredients and innovative flavor combinations.

At The Grilled Cheese Co., we are passionate about providing our customers with a warm and welcoming experience. We believe that good food is not just about taste, but also about the atmosphere and the people you share it with.

So whether you're in the mood for a classic grilled cheese sandwich or want to try something new and unique, stop by The Grilled Cheese Co. and indulge in a delicious meal. We promise you won't be disappointed!

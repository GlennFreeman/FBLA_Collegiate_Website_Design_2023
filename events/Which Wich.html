Which Wich?
5.13.23 | 1:00PM - 9:00PM

Which Wich? is a food truck event that celebrates the art of sandwich-making. Featuring a variety of food trucks, each specializing in their own unique style of sandwiches, Which Wich? brings together some of the best mobile eateries in the business. From classic deli-style sandwiches to creative, gourmet options, there is something for everyone at Which Wich?. So grab your friends and family, come hungry, and prepare to indulge in some of the most delicious and innovative sandwiches around!

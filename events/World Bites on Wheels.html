World Bites on Wheels
5.27.23 | 1:00PM - 9:00PM

World Bites on Wheels is a one-of-a-kind food truck event that takes you on a culinary journey around the globe. Featuring a wide range of international cuisines from Asia, Europe, and the Americas, this event promises to satisfy your taste buds with the most authentic and mouth-watering dishes. Whether you're in the mood for savory street food or sweet treats, you'll find it all here. So come join us for a gastronomic adventure that will leave you craving for more!

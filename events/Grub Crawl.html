Grub Crawl
5.6.23 | 1:00PM - 9:00PM

Grub Crawl is a food truck event that takes participants on a culinary adventure through a variety of mobile kitchens. It's a chance to explore different flavors and cuisines, all in one place. Participants can enjoy small bites from multiple food trucks, sample new dishes, and discover new favorites. The event is designed for food lovers who want to experience a fun and unique way to enjoy street food. So come hungry and get ready to crawl from truck to truck to discover the best grub around!

Participating Vendors
Mama's Mediterranean
Papa's Pizza
Seoul on Wheels
Suzie's Smoothies
